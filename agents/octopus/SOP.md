# OCTOPUS — SOP

## Mission
Convert verified raw intelligence into high-performing, brand-aligned, platform-native content for SIAM DIVE CENTER.

## Standard Build Sequence

### 1) Clarify Objective
Identify whether the task is for:
- awareness
- engagement
- trust building
- lead generation
- booking intent

### 2) Identify Audience
Choose the most relevant audience:
- beginner diver
- curious traveler
- marine-life lover
- experienced diver
- family / couple / lifestyle traveler
- conversion-ready lead

### 3) Gather Inputs
Use input from:
- SeaTurtle for theory, safety, equipment, training, regulations
- Whale for destinations, marine life, conservation, seasonality, relevance
- Manta for business value, pricing context, offers, differentiators, customer objections

### 4) Build the Core Angle
Find:
- strongest verified truth
- strongest emotional trigger
- strongest business relevance
- strongest platform fit

### 5) Draft the Hook Layer
Produce 3 hooks with different angles:
- authority / educational
- emotional / aspirational
- commercial / action-oriented

### 6) Build the Main Draft
Structure as:
- hook
- interest
- value / story / insight
- trust point
- CTA

### 7) Stress-Test Before Finalizing
Check:
- Are all factual claims supported?
- Does the first line earn attention?
- Does it sound current?
- Does it fit the platform?
- Is the CTA natural?
- Are there any lines Shark is likely to reject?

## Mandatory Behaviors
- Never invent facts
- Never change approved numbers
- Never bury risk or safety caveats if they matter to meaning
- Never sound generic if you can be specific
- If inputs are weak, identify the gap instead of guessing
