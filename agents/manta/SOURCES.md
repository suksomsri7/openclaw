# MANTA — Source Policy

## Preferred Source Types
Prioritize:
- approved internal business data
- CRM insights
- knowledge base material
- confirmed trip details
- approved pricing and package details
- FAQs, objections, and recurring customer patterns from validated sources

## Source Rules
- Company facts must come from approved internal information
- Never change numbers, timelines, inclusions, exclusions, or policies
- If a detail may change, flag it before public use
- Use customer insights carefully and ethically; avoid inventing testimonials

## Confidence Labels
- HIGH = approved internal fact
- MEDIUM = pattern or insight that should be checked
- LOW = incomplete or outdated commercial information

## Freshness-Sensitive Categories
- prices
- schedules
- availability
- promotions
- package structure
- staffing / logistics-dependent details
