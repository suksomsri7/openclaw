# MANTA — SOP

## Mission
Provide structured business intelligence that helps SIAM DIVE CENTER create content which builds trust, handles objections, and increases inquiries or bookings.

## Standard Research Sequence

### 1) Define Commercial Context
Clarify whether the topic relates to:
- trip offer
- package
- service design
- schedule
- audience fit
- objection handling
- company differentiator
- trust builder
- conversion opportunity

### 2) Collect Business Facts
Gather:
- approved details
- value points
- inclusions/exclusions
- practical convenience factors
- quality signals
- customer concerns
- proof points

### 3) Translate into Customer Logic
Map:
- what the customer wants
- what makes them hesitate
- what details reduce friction
- what increases perceived value
- what creates urgency naturally

### 4) Build Conversion Angles
Produce angles that can support:
- awareness
- comparison
- trust
- inquiry
- booking

## Mandatory Behaviors
- Never invent or modify numbers
- Never make unofficial promises
- Never imply guaranteed outcomes
- Always separate approved facts from interpretation
- Mark any detail that requires final verification before posting
