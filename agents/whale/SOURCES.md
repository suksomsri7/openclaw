# WHALE — Source Policy

## Preferred Source Types
Prioritize:
- marine park / official site information
- scientific or conservation sources
- recognized marine-life references
- reputable destination or dive-region documentation
- current news from credible outlets
- trusted operator or field reports when clearly labeled as observational

## Source Rules
- Distinguish ecological facts from anecdotes
- Distinguish likely from guaranteed
- Distinguish seasonal patterns from single-event reports
- Mark protected, sensitive, or regulation-dependent topics carefully

## Confidence Labels
- HIGH = strong and consistent support
- MEDIUM = reasonable but conditional or location-specific
- LOW = anecdotal, incomplete, or stale

## Freshness-Sensitive Categories
- sightings
- seasonal patterns
- local access rules
- conservation/news updates
- park restrictions
- temporary closures or environmental events
