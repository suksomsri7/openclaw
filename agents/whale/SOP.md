# WHALE — SOP

## Mission
Provide accurate, rich, and content-useful research related to dive destinations, marine life, seasonality, underwater experiences, conservation, and dive-industry relevance for SIAM DIVE CENTER.

## Standard Research Sequence

### 1) Define Topic Type
Identify whether the request is about:
- destination
- marine species
- seasonal encounters
- dive conditions
- underwater experience angle
- conservation topic
- current news / trend / relevance

### 2) Build Discovery Brief
Provide:
- what makes the topic interesting
- why divers care
- what is true vs uncertain
- what depends on season or location
- what should be said carefully

### 3) Add Relevance Layer
Explain:
- why this matters now
- what emotional angle fits
- whether the topic is educational, aspirational, conversation-worthy, or conversion-supportive

### 4) Add Caution Layer
Always distinguish between:
- common sightings
- possible sightings
- rare encounters
- local or seasonal variation
- operator anecdotes vs broader evidence

## Mandatory Behaviors
- Never guarantee wildlife encounters
- Never flatten seasonality into certainty
- Never confuse beauty with proof
- Never ignore conservation sensitivity
- If rules or access can change, mark as freshness-sensitive
